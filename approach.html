APPROACH

DISCOVER

We use questionnaires and take interviews to analyze your existing infrastructure – compute, network, storage, and backup to perform a detailed gap analysis to determine the strengths and weaknesses; then design a solution that suits your business needs. Whatever your objective may be – reduce operating cost, avoid downtime, improve the quality of service – we provide the plan and tools required for your business to run at its full potential.

DESIGN

With the data gathered in the discovery phase, we work hand in hand with your team to understand your objectives. We conduct discussions around private, public, and hybrid cloud options. Synectiks engineers are experienced in planning and designing an enhanced infrastructure to meet your business areas and ensures that the innovative solution will integrate seamlessly and operate properly in your current environment. Our experience and proficiency have given us the how to analyze and assess all the options available and isolate the right solution to implement. Workshop and proof-of-concept testing ensure successful project delivery. Our partnerships with the industry’s leading technology vendor’s allow us to design a solution that is best for your company’s needs.

Implement

Our engineering team works with you to improve a strategy based on your organizational structure, security requirements, risks, timelines, and financial requirements and maps different solutions to those needs. We compare, analyze, and evaluate your current IT environment against other best-in-class architectures and possible designs to maximize performance and attain a greater return on your investment. Our consultative approach, with a Team of Project Management, Architects, and Implementation Specialists, we design and deliver a solution that helps your company gain your business objectives.
