Government Solution

Government Solution:

Government at all levels — federal, state, and local — face obligations demanding higher IT efficiency and optimization. The need of the hour is to:

  • Boost performance, efficiency, and governance by consolidating data centers via virtualization and cloud solutions
  • Enable anywhere, anytime access to resources by both citizens and government workers with mobile computing and apps
  • Provide the large-scale data storage and backup/recovery services that underpin “big data” behind improved, real-time decision-making.
  • Ensure data, network, and access security capabilities scale seamlessly without adding unnecessary cost or complexity

Synectiks Govt Solutions offerings include:

Government Cloud

Most government data centers use as little as 2% of their server capacity, Our leading-edge Cloud/Managed Services infrastructure, available via a coast-to-coast network of 24/7 service delivery centers, provides the environment you need to hike data center performance while measuring usage, improving auditing and resource sharing — all without sacrificing security.

Mobility

As citizens and government workers alike opt for mobile devices, we help you to manage it all with mobile device management service. We help you keep mobile data safe from theft or compromise with data loss prevention solutions.

Security & data protection

Deploy the capabilities necessary to protect confidential data both in transit and in storage with our Managed Security services. Our application, network, wireless monitoring, and management services will proactively alert you when there is a chance of occurring a problem.

Data backup/replication

It has never been easier to back up your applications and fast-growing volumes of data. We can integrate real-time data replication, secure data storage, and quick-restore capabilities that cost 50%- to-60% less than most older alternatives.

Business continuity/disaster recovery

Ensure the availability and resilience of your systems, networks, applications, and data. Our experts can customize our business continuity/disaster recovery capabilities to precisely match your needs.

Unified communications

Simplify your organization’s communication technologies with our Unified IP Communications, which can cut costs and dramatically simplify provisioning, management, and maintenance.

Desktop virtualization

Ease the burden of manually maintaining PCs with our virtual desktop solutions — either desktops-as-a-service (DaaS) or virtualized desktop infrastructure (VDI) — that reduce desktop costs while simultaneously improving security.

Hosted HD Videoconferencing

Make face-to-face collaboration over long distances with ease and affordable to deploy with our Cloud HD Videoconferencing capabilities.

Custom application development

Want to build social media apps to better connect with your staff and the outside world? It is possible with our Custom Application Development Cloud; now you have the app dev environment, and you need to create them.

Customize the IT capabilities you need — with Synectiks

Combine the services and solutions that Synectiks provides into a single, customized service-level agreement (SLA) that empowers you to specify what you want (virtualization, storage, servers, network equipment, and VoIP to complex application environments and more) while also improving governance.
